# Grimm's Bluff Reporting

## register

product

## Product Purpose

Internal reporting dashboard for **Grimm's Bluff** winery operations. Aggregates WineDirect commerce data, inventory, product performance, and Tock tour analytics into views operators use for daily decisions.

## Users

- Winery owners and executives reviewing revenue, orders, and channel mix
- Tasting room and operations staff checking inventory and tour bookings
- Small team; trusted internal access, not a customer-facing product

## Brand & Tone

- **Elegant and grounded**, like a well-run estate winery: confident, warm, never flashy
- Data-first clarity over decoration; numbers should feel trustworthy
- Professional copy; no snark toward vendors in the UI

## Brand reference

- Public site: [grimmsbluff.events](https://www.grimmsbluff.events)
- Shared styles: `grimms-bluff-wines/Reference/gb-styles.css` (dark bg, gold accent, cream type)

## Anti-references

- Generic teal/cyan "SaaS analytics" palettes (previous reporting UI)
- Dark neon crypto dashboards
- Navy-and-gold fintech clichés
- Playful startup illustration overload
- Identical icon-in-card KPI grids

## Strategic principles

- Respect the user's time: filters and date range stay visible and predictable
- Charts and tables share one visual language across pages
- Logo and name anchor the experience; the tool should feel like Grimm's Bluff, not a template
